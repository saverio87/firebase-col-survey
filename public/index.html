If you’re here, you’re likely teaching in China—or considering it—and looking for answers to the same questions many of us have: “How much should I earn in City X?” “Can I save in City Y?” “What’s rent like in Z?” After years of seeing these questions repeated on Reddit and WeChat, I decided to collect real experiences and cost-of-living insights from teachers like you.

This survey aims to create a snapshot of life for teachers across China, helping others make informed decisions about moving cities, negotiating contracts, or planning their finances. Your privacy matters—feel free to use a nickname, skip questions, or provide rough estimates instead of exact figures.

The survey is a bit long, so I truly appreciate your time. In two weeks, I’ll share the compiled results to benefit everyone. Thank you for helping us all navigate teaching in China!
